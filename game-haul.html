Gamehaul - Games eCommerce app

Gamehaul is an e-commerce app designed for gamers looking to enhance their gaming experience by offering a wide selection of games, equipments and much more. The site encourages users to explore and purchase products across four key categories: hardware, peripherals, accessories, and apparel, with personalised recommendations and streamlined navigation.

Project Overview

Challenge

In the rapidly growing gaming industry, enthusiasts often face challenges when trying to purchase gaming equipment. Despite the abundance of online platforms, gamers encounter issues related to the unavailability of a dedicated and user-friendly solution tailored to their specific needs.

Goals

The aim is to design an intuitive and seamless shopping experience that caters to the unique preferences and requirements of gamers. This involves simplifying the browsing process, providing relevant product information, and offering personalised recommendations.

Research

After conducting user interviews and empathy mapping, I've gained insights into the needs and frustrations of gamers when it comes to purchasing games and consoles. Many gamers are passionate about gaming, but they often encounter difficulties in finding the latest releases and deals. They express frustration with the fragmented nature of information, having to check multiple sources to stay updated on new releases and discounts. This process can be time-consuming and overwhelming, leading some gamers to give up on finding what they're looking for. Additionally, gamers want a streamlined and convenient way to purchase games and consoles, preferably with the ability to buy directly from their smartphones. They seek a platform that offers an intuitive browsing experience, clear product information, and easy checkout options to make their gaming purchases hassle-free.

Competitive analysis

After learning more about the game industry, I wanted to take a closer look at my competitors and how they’re providing their customers with the correct information. Through my market research, I identified some top direct and indirect competitors within the industry such as:

  • CEX
  • GAME
  • Amazon
  • ASOS

Direct competitors are game e-commerce sites similar to Gamehaul, while indirect competitors aren’t focused solely on games but have a wide variety products. Exploring each of their apps and website, I evaluated the strengths and weaknesses of each to see how Gamehaul could fill in any gaps moving forward.

Project Goals

I want to enhance the user experience by designing an intuitive, visually engaging interface that makes browsing, searching, and purchasing games seamless. Ensure mobile responsiveness and accessibility so the app is inclusive and optimised across all devices. Design a streamlined and secure checkout process to reduce cart abandonment and increase user satisfaction.

Storyboard & paper wireframe

Storyboarding allows me to visually plan out scenes, helping to organise the flow of the project. By sketching my paper wireframes, I aimed to conceptualise the design and pinpoint the key challenges it needed to tackle. When drafting the homepage wireframe, my objective was to incorporate all essential information that users would require upon arriving at the website.

Digital wireframe

Digital wireframes serve as the blueprint for the gaming e-commerce website, outlining the layout and functionality of key elements before detailed design and development. These wireframes ensure a user-friendly interface, with intuitive navigation and streamlined purchasing processes. By visualising the structure, I can focus on enhancing the user experience and efficiently meeting the needs of the diverse customer base.

Final Design

The final design of the gaming e-commerce website features a sleek, intuitive interface with streamlined navigation to enhance user experience. Personalised recommendation algorithms suggest products based on individual preferences, purchase history, and browsing behavior, ensuring users can easily find items tailored to their needs. Key functionalities include profile creation, favourite items, and real-time personalised offers and discounts to boost engagement and satisfaction. The design balances aesthetic appeal with user-centric functionality, creating a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for gamers.
